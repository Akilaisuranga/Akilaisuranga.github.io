// about me

Nice to Meet You!

Hi, I'm Akila Isuranga — a passionate cybersecurity student with a strong interest in ethical hacking and penetration testing.

I am currently following the Red Team Analyst certification course at CyberWarFare Labs, where I am learning real-world attack techniques and how professional penetration testers operate in modern environments.

I have successfully completed the TryHackMe Junior Penetration Tester path, which helped me build a solid foundation in areas like information gathering, scanning, and exploitation.

I actively participate in inter-university CTF competitions and regularly practice on TryHackMe and Hack The Box. These platforms help me sharpen problem-solving skills and gain hands-on experience with real-world cybersecurity challenges.

I have worked with tools such as Nmap, Burp Suite, Gobuster, and browser developer tools to analyze web applications and identify vulnerabilities. I have practical experience with SQL Injection, XSS, and authentication bypass techniques.

I strongly believe in continuous learning, consistency, and hands-on practice — because in cybersecurity, real skills matter the most.
